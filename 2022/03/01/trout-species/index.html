Trout Species

Published Tue, Mar 1, 2022 by RJ Cody Markelz

Trout Image

Introduction

In this post we will look at some spatial data of Rainbow Trout (Oncorhynchus mykiss) and one of thier food sources Blue Winged Olives (Baetis tricaudatus).

Load the libraries

library(rgbif)
library(tidyverse)
library(data.table)
library(maps)
library(leaflet)

Pull in the species observation data from GBIF.

See post here and here for other examples of species occurance data.

rb_trout <- occ_data(scientificName = "Oncorhynchus mykiss", hasCoordinate = TRUE, limit = 10000)
bw_olive <- occ_data(scientificName = "Baetis tricaudatus", hasCoordinate = TRUE, limit = 10000)
